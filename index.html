Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to design the e-cards?

Typically, we deliver custom-designed e-cards within 2-3 business days. For rush orders, we offer 24-hour turnaround.

Can I customize the design of my invitation card?

Absolutely! Our Premium and Exclusive packages include full customization options. You can choose colors, fonts, images, and layouts.

What if guests don't confirm receipt?

With our Standard and higher packages, we make confirmation calls to guests who don't respond to the e-card message.

How does the QR code scanning work?

Each e-card contains a unique QR code. On event day, we provide scanning devices to verify guest arrival instantly. Available with our Exclusive package.

Are the e-cards secure and spam-free?

Yes! We use secure WhatsApp and SMS channels directly. No spam, no third-party involvement. Your guest data is kept confidential.

Can I send reminder messages before the event?

Yes! Premium and Exclusive packages include automatic reminder SMS messages sent to guests 1 day before your event.
